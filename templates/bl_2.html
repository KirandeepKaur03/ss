What Is an Inverter Battery?

Within the life of an inverter,battery plays a critical role. Inverter converts the grid supplied energy into DC power and battery is a device where this DC power is stored. In the event of power outage, inverter draws power from battery and inverts it back to AC form to run all household loads.

3. GEL Batteries

Compared to a standard lead-acid battery, lead acid Gel batteries provide a more stable power backup for a longer period. These have a special crisscross gel structure which retains the acid and stops it from settling down. They also have more backup power per cycle in comparison to standard flooded lead acid battery. However because of advanced components inside, GEL batteries are marginally priced higher than standard flooded lead acid battery.

Tubular Plate Batteries A tubular plate Lead acid battery consists of positive and negative plates which are having specific geometric shape. Positive plates of these batteries are having elements of tubular shape, whereas the negative plates are flat shaped.

The geometric structure of positive plates is tubular and it retains active chemical components within the inverter battery with extra support. These plates have a tubular support structure around the reactive chemical that ensures the receptive element stays in place. They are known as the best inverter batteries for areas that face prolonged power cuts like tier 2 and 3 metropolitan cities. Battery Capacity:

A battery's capacity is the sum of wattage to be expected from a battery during run time. In Ah (Ampere hours), it is measured. Like this, battery capacity is determined [Power (in watts) X back up hours (in hrs.)] / Battery Voltage (in volts) For example, buying a lead acid battery for inverter with a running time of 3 hours and assuming that the normal battery voltage is 12 V would require a battery with a capacity of (400 * 3) / 12 = 100 Ah. Batteries ranging from 100 Ah to 220 Ah are available on the market with different capacities. So how do you decide which one suits your needs best?
